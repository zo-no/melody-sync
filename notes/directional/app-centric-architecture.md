# App-Centric Architecture

Historical note only.

The older direction treated Apps as a broader policy layer that could also back public share and visitor entry surfaces. That product path is retired.

## Current interpretation

- Apps remain useful.
- Apps are owner-side reusable templates.
- Apps may shape startup context, tool defaults, and welcome text.
- Apps are not public entry points.
- Apps are not a replacement for auth or publication models.
